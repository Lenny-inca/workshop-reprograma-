Born to race

From his very first seat time in a pedal kart at the tender age of three, he showed a passion and talent for speed. Whether it’s a Formula One car, road car, motorbike, kart, pushbike, snowmobile or pair of skis - Kimi has only one speed: flat out.

The flying finn

Kimi returned to Formula One for the 2012 season, signing a two-year contract with the popular Lotus team. His return proved without doubt that he hadn’t forgotten how to drive an F1 car. He was the only driver to finish every race and scored points in all but one; his season including seven podiums, a historic win and third in the drivers’ championship. It marked a highly successful and much longed-for return to the F1 cockpit, reigniting the passion of the Iceman’s fans across the globe.
